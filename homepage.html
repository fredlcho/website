About me

I was never the most academic, or even dedicated student. I would do homework, casually study, and call it a day. However, through time, I've come to realize that if one was to be successful, this mindset could never grant that success. A passion needed to be developed. That is why I now dedicate every day to learning, no matter how big or small. In today's day and age, there is nothing short of that you can't find on the internet. I write this to you, the reader, as well as to myself in the future, as a reminder that knowledge is power, and the only thing stopping you is yourself.
-Frederick Ho
